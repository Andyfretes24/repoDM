KEVIN DI SERNA

Next on Dmensions

Genre: Progressive

Kevin Di Serna, a pampas DJ who makes himself heard in the world, with his music "conscious and from the heart".The DJ earned his place within electronic music nationwide.
For fans of the genre, it needs no introduction, for those who aren't, maybe it does. Kevin, a DJ and producer by vocation, was born in La Pampa in the late eighties. Far from appearances, showing off, and digital marketing that could clearly be the outbreak of his most popular fame, Kevin tries to show himself as he is and not fall into "ego traps."
He has worked with the most important record labels on the global scene, such as Innervisions, Sudbeat Music, Watergate, Exit Strategy and All Day I Dream, he has performed in cities such as Mexico City, Dubai, Beirut, Barcelona, ​​Ibiza, Amsterdam, New Caledonia and Tel Aviv and worked alongside progressive house figures such as Hernan Cattaneo, Nick Warren, Dixon and Sasha, says that all these trips and experiences are "consequences and seasonings" of his "clear" intention to work doing what he truly "loves"

About Us


Know more about Dmensions

Dimensions Music Event is a itinerant cycle for the electronic music industry in the North of Argentina.
In this space, the new generation of artists and professionals of electronic music, can be inspired and show their work. In addition, our events offers high-quality contributions to current topics of the scene and is also an ideal place for networking between artists and established players in the industry.
