About Dmensions

Get to know us

Dimensions Music Event is a itinerant cycle for the electronic music industry in the North of Argentina.
In this space, the new generation of artists and professionals of electronic music, can be inspired and show their work. In addition, our events offers high-quality contributions to current topics of the scene and is also an ideal place for networking between artists and established players in the industry.

You can find all kinds of music genre in Dmensions...
TECHNO, PROGRESSIVE, HOUSE, TRANCE, AND MUCH MORE...

Every cycle is different, every artist is unique.
Every experience will blow your mind
